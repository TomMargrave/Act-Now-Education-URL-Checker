Platinum Edge partners with the Scrum Alliance to provide heavily discounted (or free if unemployed) seats for veterans in our certified scrum classes (CSM, CSPO, A-CSM), aimed at improving their project and product management skills and making them more competitive in the job market. The Certified ScrumMaster (CSM), Certified Scrum Product Owner (CSPO), Advanced Certified ScrumMaster (A-CSM) classes, which Platinum Edge normally sells for over $1,000 each, are offered to veterans only for $99, and free for unemployed veterans (with an unemployment benefits statement). Each class satisfies the Scrum Alliance learning objectives and qualifies for certification and two-year membership with Scrum Alliance. Please note this offer can only be redeemed once per person and cannot be combined with any other offers.
